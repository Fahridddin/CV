U-Feel true emotions detecting project

UFeel is a project designed to analyze people's emotions and provide accurate results. This innovative technology can be seamlessly integrated into social media platforms, offering a more nuanced way for users to express their sentiments instead of relying on conventional like and dislike buttons.

Moreover, UFeel holds promising applications in the field of education, enabling educators to gauge students' emotional responses during lectures and gain insights into their comprehension levels.

Setellite imagery based project

Our satellite imagery-based project utilizes cutting-edge technology to monitor environmental changes, track urban development, and aid disaster response efforts with unprecedented accuracy.
